Class: Chair

Attributes:
	1. Wheels (boolean)
	2. Arm Rests (boolean)
	3. Adjustable_height (boolean)
	4. Fabric (string)
	5. Frame_material (string)
	6. Color (string)
	7. Price (string)
	8. Weight (float)
	9. Weight_capacity (float)
	10. Seat_height_minimum (float)
	11. Seat_height_maximum (float)
	12. Foldable (boolean)
	13. Dimensions_setup (string)
	14. Dimensions_folded (string)
	15. Current_seat_height (float)

Methods:
	1. Raise_height (add Height_adjust to Current_seat_height)
	2. Sit (add Weight_sit to Weight and subtract Weight_sit from Weight_capacity)
	3. Stand_up (restore Weight and Weight_capactity)
	4. Lower_height (subtract Height_adjust from Current_seat_height)
	5. Mark_on_sale (multiply Price by Sale_percentage and subtract Discount from Price)
